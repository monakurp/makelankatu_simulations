ERA5-Land hourly data

Download from:
Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) (2019): C3S ERA5-Land reanalysis. Copernicus Climate Change Service, date of access: 27 Feb 2020. https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/cdsapp#!/home
